Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide service all over Turkey ?

We serve everywhere that has a pharmacy within 1 kilometer. If there is a pharmacy near you, you can safely request your medicine.

Is there a lower payment limit for placing an order ?

There is no lower limit for each of your medications. You can safely request your prescription medications from us.

How can I order ?

You can place an order after filling your personal and address information by typing your recipe code in the search box on the Home Page.

How does the system work ?

With the prescription code you received from your doctor, you can enter your information through our system and request it from your pharmacy. After we receive your medication, we deliver it to you. You can pay by credit card or at the door.
